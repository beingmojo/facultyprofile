FIRST LOGIN

For first time users a Account Information Screen is presented (shown below). On most occasions this screen would be automatically populated from the information in the UTA User directory. Complete the missing the missing information and press Enter or Submit and Proceed to My Profiles.

Details on the Account Information can be changed anytime using the Account Information Link on your My Profiles page
